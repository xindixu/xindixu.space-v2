Project Description

  • Designed an integrated communication plan to help Triobike, a Danish cargo bike company, gain Austin market
  • Researched on the brand/product profile, conducted extensive research on 3 major competitors, and concluded that test drive would distinguish Triobike from other competitors
  • Found potential target groups based on the product characteristics, narrowed it down to two segments, young parents and old grandpas, and researched on their media consumption behaviors
  • Searched for potential locations for experiential media, came up with the logistics of the events, and backed up with data found online
  • Designed social media strategy such as Instagram AR filters, that attracts target's interest to encourage engagement and purchase
